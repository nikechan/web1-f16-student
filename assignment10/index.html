Who Are We?

In 1992, Master Tsai received a mission from the Elder, Jih Der Da Di, to propagate the Dao from Taiwan to the United States. Together with the Boston Dao relatives, the Tian Ann Jih Der Temple was established. With passion and determination, Master Tsai devotes herself to spread Chinese traditional culture and Confucius teaching by preaching the ways of the Heavenly Saints to help mankind reach enlightenment. After many years of dedication, Tian Ann Temple has expanded and currently has more than twenty thousand Dao relatives, and established close to fifty family temples all over Massachusetts.

The main temple, located in Braintree, offers classes for all age groups. Classes introduce the youth to the Five Constant Virtues (benevolence, righteousness, propriety, wisdom, and fidelity), and guide the adults to incorporate these teachings into their daily life. We believe that by having a good understanding of the virtues and act in accordance to our conscience, it will help harmonize the family, benefit society, and making this world a better place.
