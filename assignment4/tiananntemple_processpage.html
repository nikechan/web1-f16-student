CLIENT INFO

  • Religious Organization and Community
  • Began in Boston/Braintree since 1994
  • Current Website: http://www.tiananntemple.org/

CLIENT NEEDS

The mentioned that they needed help on rebuilding their current website, which isn't even complete. There was also the issue of making it look clean and simple, yet modern and traditional at the same time. They just want a site that will be able to introduce their temple and spread word about their teachings and events that they hold.

Here are a few other needs that the client requested:

  • Chinese and English version (focus on English only for now)
  • simple and user-friendly
  • mix of modern and Chinese classical
  • Links and resources = sutras, songs, vegetarian recipes
